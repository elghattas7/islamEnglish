Scientific Insights in the Quran

Discovering the remarkable scientific facts mentioned in the Quran 1400 years ago

The Quran was revealed to Muhammad (peace be upon him) who was unlettered; he could neither read nor write. His people were generally unlettered as well. Yet the Quran contains accurate scientific information that was only discovered by modern science centuries later.

"Verily, in the creation of the heavens and the earth, and in the alternation of night and day, and the ships which sail through the sea with that which is of use to mankind, and the water (rain) which Allah sends down from the sky and makes the earth alive therewith after its death, and the moving (living) creatures of all kinds that He has scattered therein, and in the veering of winds and clouds which are held between the sky and the earth, are indeed Ayat (proofs, evidences, signs) for people of understanding."

‚Äî Quran 2:164

The Big Bang Theory in the Quran

"Have those who disbelieved not considered that the heavens and the earth were one single entity, which We then parted asunder and made from water every living thing? Will they not, then, believe?"

‚Äî Quran 21:30

This verse makes unmistakable reference to the fact that Allah created the universe from a single entity, which He then ordered to split. Modern astrophysicists assert that the entire universe originated from one entity, the result of what is known today as the 'Big Bang'.

Modern Science Confirms:

The Big Bang theory states that the universe began as an extremely hot and dense point roughly 13.8 billion years ago. This single point then expanded rapidly, creating space and time as we know it.

The Universe as Smoke

"Then He directed Himself to the heaven while it was smoke and said to it and to the earth, 'Come [into being], willingly or by compulsion.' They said, 'We have come willingly.'"

‚Äî Quran 41:11

This verse affirms that the sky, during its early stages was like smoke; this is also affirmed by modern science. The early universe was indeed filled with a hot, dense plasma that resembled smoke.

üöÄ The Expansion of the Universe

"And We have built the heavens with Our own Hands; and, verily, it is We who are steadily expanding it."

‚Äî Quran 51:47

These verses confirm that the universe we live in is continuously expanding in space. This was discovered by astronomers like Edwin Hubble in the 20th century, who confirmed that the universe was expanding and that the more remote the galaxy, the higher was its recession velocity.

Edwin Hubble's Discovery:

American astronomer Edwin Hubble confirmed that the universe was expanding and that the more remote the galaxy the higher was its recession velocity; and that its recession velocity was proportional to its distance.

Everything in Orbit

"And it is He Who has created the night and the day and the sun and the moon ‚Äì all of them floating through space (in their orbits)."

‚Äî Quran 21:33

In these verses Allah states that the sun is traveling towards a certain direction. Previously, it was perceived that the sun was motionless. Modern day cosmologists and astronomers have affirmed that the sun moves in a certain direction.

The Sun and Moon's Light

"It is He who made the sun a [source] of radiant light and the moon a light [reflected], and has determined for it phases so that you may know the number of years and to measure time."

‚Äî Quran 10:5

The perfect distinction made between light given off by a luminous, fiery body (the sun) and light reflected from the sun by a dark, cold body (the moon) has been mentioned in the Quran over fourteen hundred years ago!

üåç Atmospheric Science

Air Pressure at High Altitudes

"And whomsoever Allah wills to guide, He opens his breast to Islam, and whomsoever He wills to send astray, He makes his breast closed and constricted, as if he is climbing up to the sky."

‚Äî Quran 6:125

It can be concluded from this verse that the more one ascends into the atmosphere the harder it is for him to breathe. This is due to the reduction in pressure and low oxygen levels. This fact was discovered only after humans learned how to fly and reach heights.

Modern Understanding:

When a person ascends into the sky beyond eight kilometers above sea level, they encounter breathing problems due to the lack of oxygen and low air pressure. Problems include hypoxia (low oxygen reaching body tissues) and dysbarism (symptoms from low air pressure).

Darkness of Space

"And even if We had opened to them a gateway to heaven and they had ascended, on and on, up to it, they would surely say (in the evening): 'Our eyes have been (as if) dazzled (we have not seen any angel or heaven). Nay, we are a people bewitched!'"

‚Äî Quran 15:14

This verse presents a universal fact which humans previously were ignorant of, and only discovered during the age of space exploration in the sixties. The entire universe is engulfed in darkness. Light visible on earth is observed for only about two hundred kilometers, beyond that, there is complete darkness.

üíß Water and Life

"And made from water every living thing? Will they not, then, believe?"

‚Äî Quran 21:30

This verse clearly states that water is the basis of all life. Modern science has confirmed that all living things are made up largely of water and that all life processes depend on water.

Scientific Fact:

Water makes up 60-70% of the human body, and all known life forms require water to survive. Water is essential for cellular processes, metabolism, and the transport of nutrients and waste products.

"A totally objective examination of it [the Qur'an] in the light of modern knowledge, leads us to recognize the agreement between the two, as has been already noted on repeated occasions. It makes us deem it quite unthinkable for a man of Muhammad's time to have been the author of such statements, on account of the state of knowledge in his day."

Important Note

The goal in mentioning these scientific facts is not to tie every modern scientific breakthrough with a verse from the Qur'an. Rather, it is to clarify that there are verses in the Qur'an which contain accurate scientific information, which have only been discovered very recently by modern science. This affirms that the Qur'an is divine revelation from Allah.
