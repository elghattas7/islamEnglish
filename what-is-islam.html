What is Islam?

Understanding the fundamental beliefs and principles of the Islamic faith

The Meaning of Islam

Islam is to submit to Allah in His Oneness (Monotheism), to be subservient to Him in obedience and not to associate any partners, rivals, and intercessors with Him. It is a religion of tolerance and ease.

The Comprehensive Nature of Islam

Islam comprises a number of acts of worship, among which some are verbal, physical and creedal acts of worship. These acts of worship play an important role in:

  • Refining one's manners
  • Purifying one's soul
  • Reforming oneself
  • Maintaining the integrity and unity of Muslim society
